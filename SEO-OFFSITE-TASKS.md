# Just Legal Solutions — Off-Site SEO & GBP Action Plan
**Date:** April 10, 2026

This guide covers all the high-priority SEO actions that **do not** require code changes. Focus on these to turn the impressions we've gained into actual leads and revenue.

## 1. Google Business Profile (GBP) — Your Biggest Lead Driver
GBP accounts for nearly 50% of local ranking signals. Consistency is key.

### Weekly Actions
- **Post Weekly:** Treat this like Facebook or Instagram. Post a photo of a courthouse, a served document (blurred), or just a quick update ("Served 15 cases in Tulsa this week!"). 
- **Seed Q&A:** Don't wait for customers. You can add and answer questions yourself (Google encourages this). Add these this week:
  - "How much does a process server cost in Tulsa?"
  - "Do you serve papers on weekends and holidays?"
  - "How long does process serving take?"
  - "Can someone refuse to be served?"

### Monthly Actions
- **Add Photos:** Target 100+ photos total. Google rewards active profiles. 
  - Photos of you at courthouses (Tulsa, Broken Arrow, etc.)
  - Photos of your vehicle
  - Photos of service area maps
- **Review velocity:** After every successful job, send the client your GBP link. Your 156+ reviews at 5.0 is your "secret weapon" — don't let it stale.

## 3. Ongoing Content Strategy
Google rewards "freshness" and depth.

- **1 Blog Post per Week:** Focus on long-tail questions users are actually asking (based on Google Search Console data).
  - *Topic 1:* "How to become a process server in Oklahoma (2026 Guide)"
  - *Topic 2:* "What happens if someone hides from a process server in Tulsa?"
  - *Topic 3:* "Eviction notices in Oklahoma City: The 2026 Guide"
- **Update Existing Content:** Every 3 months, change a few paragraphs or add a new FAQ to your top pages. This tells Google the site is active and accurate.
