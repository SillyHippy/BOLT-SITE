# Service of Process in Oklahoma: Rush vs Standard vs Same-Day

**Understanding your service options helps you choose the right speed for your legal needs and budget.**

When you need legal documents served in Oklahoma, timing can be everything. A defendant about to leave the state, a witness scheduled to move, or a court deadline looming—all create urgency that demands different service speeds. This guide explains the differences between standard, rush, and same-day process serving in Oklahoma, helping you make the right choice for your situation.

Just Legal Solutions offers three service tiers to match your timeline and budget. Understanding what each tier includes helps you balance urgency with cost.

## How to Choose the Right Service Speed

### Decision Matrix:

**Choose STANDARD When:**
- Court date is 3+ weeks away
- Defendant is cooperative
- Budget is primary concern
- Case is routine/non-urgent
- No specific deadline pressure

**Choose RUSH When:**
- Court date is 1-2 weeks away
- You need results this week
- Defendant may become unavailable
- Opposing counsel is pushing timeline
- Discovery deadlines approaching

**Choose SAME-DAY When:**
- Emergency court order
- Defendant leaving town today/tomorrow
- TRO or protective order
- Statute expiring
- Asset at risk
- Same-day court deadline

### Questions to Ask Yourself:

1. **When is my court date?**
   - More than 2 weeks → Standard
   - 1-2 weeks → Rush
   - Less than 1 week → Same-Day

2. **Is the defendant likely to avoid service?**
   - Cooperative → Standard
   - May avoid → Rush
   - Actively avoiding → Same-Day

3. **What's my budget?**
   - Tight → Standard
   - Moderate → Rush
   - Urgent need → Same-Day

4. **What's at stake?**
   - Routine matter → Standard
   - Important deadline → Rush
   - Emergency situation → Same-Day

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Can I upgrade from standard to rush or same-day?

**A:** Yes. If standard service is in progress and you need faster results, contact us to upgrade. You'll pay the difference in pricing, and we'll immediately prioritize your case.

**A:** For rush and same-day service, if we don't complete service due to our limitations (not defendant avoidance), we refund the difference to standard service rates and continue attempts at no additional charge.

### Q: Are there any hidden fees?

Additional charges only apply for:

### Q: Do you offer discounts for multiple cases?

**A:** Yes. For attorneys and businesses with regular service needs, we offer volume discounts:

### Q: Is same-day service really same-day?

**A:** We guarantee service within 24 hours for same-day tier. In most cases, we complete service the same day the order is received, but circumstances (defendant availability, location, etc.) can affect timing. We prioritize your case and work until service is complete or 24 hours pass.

### Q: What counties do you cover?

**A:** We serve all 77 Oklahoma counties. Standard and rush service rates apply to Tulsa, Creek, Wagoner, Rogers, and Osage counties. Extended travel beyond these counties may incur additional charges quoted upfront.

### Q: Can I get a refund if the case settles before service?

**A:** If service has not been attempted, we offer full refunds. If one or more attempts have been made, we prorate based on work completed.

## Why Speed Matters in Legal Service

### Court Deadline Considerations:

Most Oklahoma courts require service to be completed:
- **14 days** before hearings (standard matters)
- **72 hours** before emergency hearings
- **180 days** from filing (absolute deadline under 12 O.S. § 2005)

Missing deadlines can result in:
- Continuances (delays)
- Dismissal of your case
- Loss of legal rights
- Additional filing fees
- Opposing counsel sanctions

### Strategic Advantages of Fast Service:

**Element of Surprise:**
- Faster service catches defendants before they can avoid
- Prevents assets from being hidden
- Preserves evidence
- Maintains legal advantage

**Case Momentum:**
- Keeps your case moving forward
- Demonstrates seriousness to opposing party
- Meets discovery deadlines
- Allows faster resolution

**Client Satisfaction:**
- Reduces client anxiety
- Shows responsiveness
- Builds trust
- Demonstrates competence
