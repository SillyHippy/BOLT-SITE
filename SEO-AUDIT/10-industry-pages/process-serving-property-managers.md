## Comprehensive Property Management Process Serving Services

Oklahoma's Residential Landlord and Tenant Act, specifically 41 O.S. § 131, establishes strict requirements for eviction notice service. Our process servers are thoroughly trained in these statutory requirements, ensuring your notices are served properly and your eviction cases proceed smoothly.

**Eviction notice services include:**

- **Five-Day Notices**: Service of non-payment of rent notices required before filing eviction actions
- **Ten-Day Notices**: Service of notice to quit for lease violations and other grounds
- **Thirty-Day Notices**: Service of termination notices for month-to-month tenancies
- **Immediate Termination Notices**: Service for lease violations that permit immediate termination
- **Notice of Rent Increase**: Service of required notices for rent adjustments

We understand the timing requirements of Oklahoma eviction law and provide prompt service to minimize the timeline for removing problem tenants and returning your properties to revenue-generating status.

### Forcible Entry and Detainer (FED) Service

When eviction proceedings reach the litigation stage, proper service of the forcible entry and detainer petition and summons is critical. Our team ensures compliance with both 41 O.S. § 131 and 12 O.S. § 2004, providing service that supports prompt court action and tenant removal.

**FED process serving includes:**

## Skip Tracing for Evasive Tenants

### Finding Tenants Who Skip Out

Tenants facing eviction often attempt to avoid service by moving without notice, providing false forwarding addresses, or otherwise becoming difficult to locate. Our integrated skip tracing services locate these tenants so service can be completed and eviction proceedings can continue.

**Skip tracing for property management:**

- **Address Verification**: Confirming current residence of tenants who have moved
- **Employment Location**: Finding current employers for workplace service attempts
- **Associate Tracing**: Using family and associate connections to locate tenants
- **Vehicle Registration**: Locating tenants through registered vehicles
- **Digital Investigation**: Social media and online presence analysis

### Dealing with Intentionally Evasive Tenants

Some tenants actively work to avoid service, hiding from process servers and refusing to answer doors. Our servers employ proven strategies for serving these difficult cases:

- **Strategic Timing**: Service attempts at times when evasive tenants are likely to be present
- **Staking Strategies**: Coordinated surveillance to intercept tenants leaving properties
- **Neighbor Intelligence**: Professional inquiries with neighbors and property staff
- **Workplace Service**: Employment-based service when residential service fails
- **Substituted Service**: Alternative service methods when permitted by law
