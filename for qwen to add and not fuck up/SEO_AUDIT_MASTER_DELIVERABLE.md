### 2. Should process serving and notary have separate hubs?

**YES, absolutely.** Currently notary is treated as secondary. These should be equal pillars:
- `/process-serving/` — Hub for all serving content
- `/notary-services/` — Hub for all notary content (expand from current `/notary/`)
- City pages should have BOTH: `/service-areas/[city]/process-serving` AND `/service-areas/[city]/mobile-notary`
