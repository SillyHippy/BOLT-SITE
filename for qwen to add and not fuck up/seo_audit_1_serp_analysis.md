## Ranking Patterns

### Process Server Queries

**Page Type Dominance:**
- **Homepages dominate** for brand-focused queries ("tulsa process server", "process server tulsa ok")
- **Directory listings** (ServeNow, NAPPS, OKPPSA) appear frequently for generic terms
- **National brand pages** (ABC Legal) rank for "hire process server" commercial intent queries
- **Location pages** (notaryintulsa.com model) rank for suburb-specific queries

**Title Tag Patterns:**
1. **Local + Service + Phone:** "Process Server in Tulsa | [Brand] | (918) ###-####" - Precision Process Serving model
2. **State + Service + Phone:** "Oklahoma Service of Process | Process Server | ###-###-####" - Oklahoma Service of Process model
3. **Service + City + Modifier:** "Tulsa Process Server | Same Day Service | [Brand]"
4. **Geo-specific location pages:** "Mobile Notary in [City], OK | [Brand]" - On-the-Go Notary model

**Common Content Structures:**
1. **Hero section:** Service headline + phone number + CTA button
2. **Trust bar:** Licensed/Bonded/Insured badges + years in business
3. **Service grid:** 3-6 service cards (Process Service, Skip Tracing, Court Filing, Notary)
4. **Coverage area list:** Bullet or paragraph listing served cities/counties
5. **Testimonial section:** 2-4 client quotes (ideally with names/star ratings)
6. **FAQ accordion:** 4-8 common questions (pricing, timeline, service area)
7. **Contact section:** Form + phone + email + physical address

**Local Pack vs Organic Results:**
- **Local Pack (Map 3-Pack):** Dominated by businesses with:
  - Google Business Profile optimization
  - Physical addresses (not P.O. Boxes)
  - Review quantity and recency
  - Category precision ("Process Server" primary)
  - Proximity to searcher

- **Organic Results:** Mix of:
  - Established independent websites (Oklahoma Service of Process)
  - Directory listings (ServeNow, NAPPS, Yelp)
  - National providers (ABC Legal)
  - Review sites (Yelp, Google Business Profile pages)

### Notary Queries

**Page Type Dominance:**
- **Location pages** heavily dominate suburb-specific queries ("mobile notary broken arrow", "notary sapulpa")
- **Homepages** rank for broad "mobile notary tulsa" queries
- **Service pages** rank for specific needs ("loan signing tulsa", "hospital notary tulsa")

**Title Tag Patterns:**
1. **Service + City + OK:** "Mobile Notary in [City], OK | On-the-Go Notary in Tulsa"
2. **City + Mobile Notary:** "[City] Mobile Notary - [Brand] - Phone"
3. **Service + Type + Location:** "24/7 Mobile Notary Service | Tulsa | [Brand]"

**Common Content Structures:**
1. **Service explanation:** What mobile notary means, travel to client
2. **Document types list:** Power of Attorney, Wills, Real Estate, Medical
3. **Pricing transparency:** Flat rates ($50 standard, $75 after-hours)
4. **Coverage radius map:** Visual or text list of served areas
5. **RON (Remote Online Notarization) section:** Oklahoma RON legality explanation
6. **ID requirements:** What to bring to appointment
7. **Booking options:** Phone, text, email, online booking

**Local Pack vs Organic Results:**
- **Local Pack:** Heavily favors businesses with:
  - "Mobile Notary" as primary or secondary GBP category
  - High review volume (especially Google)
  - Service area radius properly configured
  - Photo optimization on GBP

- **Organic Results:** 
  - Individual notary websites (notaryintulsa.com model)
  - National notary directories (123notary, Notary Rotary)
  - Signing service platforms (Snapdocs, Notary Cafe)
  - General service directories (Yelp, Thumbtack)

### Geographic Strategy Insights

**What's Working:**
1. **Individual location pages** for each suburb (On-the-Go Notary model) - ranks for "[service] in [city]" queries
2. **County seat targeting** - Process servers emphasizing Creek County (Sapulpa), Rogers County (Claremore) capture county-level legal work
3. **Coverage area lists** - Even simple text lists of served cities help with geographic relevance

**Underserved Opportunities:**
1. **Wagoner County** - Minimal competition despite being adjacent to Tulsa metro
2. **Osage County** - Rural, underserved, Pawhuska as county seat
3. **Sand Springs / Glenpool** - Western suburbs with less saturation
4. **Skiatook / Collinsville** - Northern suburbs, Collinsville has Smoking Gun presence but room remains
