## Industry-Specific Strategy Notes

### For Process Serving Focus:
- **ServeNow.com** is non-negotiable - it's the industry standard directory law firms use
- **NAPPS membership** provides networking with other professionals and referral opportunities
- **Oklahoma Private Process Server Association** is essential for state credibility
- Emphasize "Oklahoma statewide coverage" in all listings

### For Mobile Notary Focus:
- **123notary, NotaryRotary, NotaryCafe** are the "Big 3" for loan signings
- **NNA certification** is often required by title companies
- **SigningAgent.com** listing comes with NNA certification package
- Emphasize "Tulsa metro + travel" and loan signing expertise

### For Legal Courier Services:
- Position as "legal courier" on general business directories
- Target **LinkedIn** for B2B connections with law firms
- **Chamber of Commerce** networking for corporate contracts
