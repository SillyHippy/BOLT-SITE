## About Midwest City

Midwest City is a suburb of Oklahoma City located in Oklahoma County. Established in 1942 as a bedroom community for Tinker Air Force Base, it has grown to become one of Oklahoma's largest cities. With a population of approximately 58,000, Midwest City benefits from its proximity to Tinker AFB and the aerospace industry.

**Key Statistics:**
- Population: ~58,000
- County: Oklahoma County
- Founded: 1942
- Major Highways: I-40, I-240, US-62, US-77
- Notable: Home to Rose State College; adjacent to Tinker AFB
