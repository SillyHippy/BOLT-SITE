## Coal County Process Server You Can Trust

Coal County earned its name from the underground mines that once drove the local economy in the late 1800s and early 1900s, and that history still shapes the area today. The county seat of **Coalgate** sits at the junction of US-75 and State Highway 3, and the surrounding country is a mix of pasture, scrub timber, and the rolling foothills that lead toward the Arbuckles to the west and the Kiamichi country to the east. With one of the smallest populations of any Oklahoma county, Coal County is a place where word travels fast and addresses do not always match what you see on a mapping app.

Just Legal Solutions covers Coal County with licensed Oklahoma process servers who understand the difference between a confirmed Coalgate residence and a pasture lease where someone "stays sometimes." We treat every Coal County job as a real assignment, not a throwaway. The Coal County Courthouse at 4 N Main St in Coalgate handles all district court matters for the county, and that is where returns of service are filed. The Coal County Court Clerk can be reached at (580) 927-2103 for case-specific questions.
