## We Serve All of Major County

Just Legal Solutions covers every town, township, and rural route in Major County. Below are the communities we work in most often.

### Primary Service Areas

- **Fairview** — The county seat and the largest community in Major County. Home to the courthouse, the hospital, and most of the county's professional offices, Fairview is where we handle the highest volume of personal service, business service on registered agents, and courthouse filings.
- **Ringwood** — A close-knit town along Highway 60 with a strong school district and an agricultural workforce; we handle service here at homes, the school district offices, and surrounding farm sites.
- **Cleo Springs** — A small farming community in the northern part of the county where evening and early-morning attempts are usually the most productive.
- **Meno** — A historic Mennonite community with quiet residential streets; respectful, low-profile service is the standard expectation here, and our servers are trained to honor that.
- **Ames** — A small unincorporated community in the southern portion of Major County where rural-route addressing is common, and local landmarks often guide the final approach.
- **Isabella** — One of the smaller communities in the county; we serve documents at homes and at nearby agricultural operations.

### Rural Major County Service

A large share of Major County is open ranch land, wheat acreage, and oil and gas country sitting just east of the Glass Mountains. Just Legal Solutions regularly serves documents at:

- Working farms and ranches reached by section roads
- Oil and gas lease sites and tank batteries
- Mailing addresses tied to rural-route delivery
- Seasonal hunting properties and weekend cabins
- Equipment yards, grain elevators, and co-op locations

Our servers use GPS-verified attempts so that even when an address is "the second gate past the curve," the record reflects exactly where and when we were on site.
