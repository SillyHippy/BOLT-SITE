## Cotton County Process Server You Can Trust

Cotton County sits in southwest Oklahoma along the Red River, just south of Comanche County and the city of Lawton. The county was carved out in 1912, named for the cash crop that once dominated the region, and it still carries the feel of small-town agricultural Oklahoma. The county seat of **Walters** is centered on US-277 and US-281, the main routes connecting Lawton to the Texas line, and most legal business in the county runs through the courthouse on North Broadway.

Just Legal Solutions covers Cotton County with experienced Oklahoma process servers who understand both the small-town pace and the cross-border realities of working a county that touches Texas. Defendants in Cotton County may live near the Red River, work in Lawton or Wichita Falls, or split time across the state line.
