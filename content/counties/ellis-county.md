## Ellis County Process Server You Can Trust

Ellis County sits in northwest Oklahoma, pressed up against the Texas Panhandle and shaped by the wheat fields, ranches, and oil-and-gas activity that define the region. The county seat of **Arnett** is a small community along US-60, and the largest town, **Shattuck**, sits a few miles east on the same highway and is best known for the Shattuck Windmill Museum and Park. The county is sparsely populated and stretches across roughly 1,230 square miles, which means addresses are often rural and distances between attempts can be significant.

Just Legal Solutions covers Ellis County with licensed Oklahoma process servers who know what it takes to serve documents in northwest Oklahoma.
