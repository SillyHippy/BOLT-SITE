## About Love County, Oklahoma

**Love County** is located in South Central Oklahoma, bordering Texas along the Red River. Established in 1907 upon Oklahoma statehood, the county was named for **Overton Love**, a prominent Chickasaw judge. The county encompasses **532 square miles** of rolling prairie and historic small towns.

## Chickasaw Nation Jurisdiction

**Critical Information:** Love County lies entirely within the historic territory and contemporary jurisdiction of the **Chickasaw Nation**, one of the Five Civilized Tribes of Oklahoma.

Process servers operating in Love County must be aware of:

- **Tribal court jurisdiction** for matters involving Chickasaw Nation citizens
- **Concurrent jurisdiction** between state and tribal courts
- **Tribal sovereignty** affecting service of process procedures

The Chickasaw Nation maintains a significant presence through the **WinStar World Casino and Resort** in Thackerville—the tribe's flagship destination and the largest employer in the region. Process servers should verify whether cases involve tribal parties, as this may affect service procedures.

## Marietta: Historic County Seat

**Marietta** serves as the governmental and commercial center of Love County. Located at the intersection of **Interstate 35** and **State Highway 32**, Marietta sits approximately **15 miles north of the Red River** and **114 miles south of Oklahoma City**.

### Historical Significance

Marietta was incorporated on **June 2, 1898**, with settlement beginning decades earlier. According to tradition, the town was named for **Marie Etta**, wife of Jerry Washington, who became Marietta's first postmaster when the post office opened on **December 20, 1887**.

The **Love County Courthouse**, constructed in 1910, represents significant historical value as Oklahoma's first post-statehood courthouse.

### Mercy Health/Love County Hospital

**Mercy Health/Love County Hospital and Clinic** serves as the primary healthcare provider for the region, employing over 200 staff members and providing essential medical services to residents and visitors.
