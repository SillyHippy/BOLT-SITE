Documents can be emailed, mailed to our Tulsa office, or uploaded through our secure client portal. We handle all preparation and service.

## About Muskogee County

Muskogee County, established in 1907, has Muskogee as its county seat. The area has significant Native American history and is located within the Muscogee (Creek) Nation reservation. The county is home to the Jack C. Montgomery VA Medical Center, one of the region's largest employers.

**Key Statistics:**
- County Seat: Muskogee
- Population: ~66,000
- Area: 814 square miles
- Major Highways: US-62, US-64, US-69, OK-16, OK-165
- Notable: Home to Jack C. Montgomery VA Medical Center; Muscogee (Creek) Nation presence
