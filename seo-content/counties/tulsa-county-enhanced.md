**Address:** 500 S. Denver Ave, Tulsa, OK 74103  
**Phone:** (918) 596-5000  
**Hours:** Monday–Friday, 8:00 AM–5:00 PM (Closes at 4:30 PM for filings)

![Tulsa County Courthouse](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/e/e8/Tulsa_County_Courthouse.jpg/1280px-Tulsa_Courthouse.jpg)  
*Tulsa County Courthouse — Historic Art Deco landmark built in 1937*  
*Source: [Wikimedia Commons](https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Tulsa_County_Courthouse.jpg)*

The Tulsa County Courthouse is a stunning example of Art Deco architecture, constructed in 1937 during the Great Depression as a Works Progress Administration project. The courthouse serves as the hub for all district court proceedings in Tulsa County and houses the Court Clerk's office where legal documents are filed. For process servers, understanding courthouse procedures, filing deadlines, and clerk requirements is essential for efficient service completion.

## Why Tulsa County is Unique

### The Oil Capital Heritage

Tulsa earned the nickname "Oil Capital of the World" during the early 20th century oil boom that transformed the region. The 1901 discovery of the Red Fork oil field and the legendary 1905 Glenn Pool strike brought unprecedented wealth and development to the area. This oil heritage continues to influence Tulsa County's economy, culture, and architecture.[^3]

### Art Deco Architecture

Tulsa boasts one of the finest collections of Art Deco architecture in the United States, second only to Miami Beach. Notable examples include:

- **Boston Avenue Methodist Church** — National Historic Landmark
- **Philtower Building** — 1928 Gothic-inspired tower
- **Tulsa County Courthouse** — 1937 WPA Art Deco masterpiece
- **Depot** — Historic 1931 train station

This architectural heritage creates unique service challenges and opportunities, from serving documents in historic high-rise buildings to navigating the distinctive street layouts of downtown Tulsa.

### Economic Significance

Tulsa County serves as the economic engine for northeastern Oklahoma:

| Industry Sector | Major Employers | Economic Impact |
|-----------------|-----------------|-----------------|
| Energy/Oil & Gas | Williams Companies, ONEOK, Magellan | Historical foundation, continued dominance |
| Aerospace | American Airlines, Spirit AeroSystems | 20,000+ jobs, $4B+ annual impact |
| Healthcare | Saint Francis Health System, Hillcrest | Largest employment sector |
| Technology | Tulsa Innovation Labs, tech startups | Rapidly growing sector |
| Manufacturing | Whirlpool, AAON, Muncie Power | Diverse industrial base |
